About Cobra Kai

Welcome to Cobra Kai Dojo, where we embody the philosophy of "Strike First. Strike Hard. No Mercy." and train students to achieve excellence through discipline and strength.

Meet Our Sensei

Johnny Lawrence

Sensei Johnny Lawrence leads Cobra Kai with a philosophy that balances aggressive offense and disciplined defense. Drawing from his own journey, he instills confidence, perseverance, and honor in his students, guiding them to become formidable martial artists.

Training Programs

Beginner Program

Designed for newcomers to martial arts, this program introduces students to the fundamentals of karate, emphasizing basic stances, strikes, and defensive techniques.

Intermediate Program

For students who have mastered the basics, this program delves deeper into technique refinement and introduces sparring elements

Advanced Program

Tailored for seasoned martial artists aiming to perfect their skills and prepare for competitions or teaching roles.

Youth Program

Aimed at children aged 7–14, this program focuses on building confidence, discipline, and physical fitness in a fun environment.
