About Cobra Kai

Welcome to Cobra Kai Dojo, where we embody the philosophy of "Strike First. Strike Hard. No Mercy." and train students to achieve excellence through discipline and strength.

Meet Our Sensei

Johnny Lawrence

Sensei Johnny Lawrence leads Cobra Kai with a philosophy that balances aggressive offense and disciplined defense. Drawing from his own journey, he instills confidence, perseverance, and honor in his students, guiding them to become formidable martial artists.
